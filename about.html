ABOUT US


MeaCopper was established in 2010 by joining a group of highly skilled employees with years of on hand experience for the sole purpose of forming a small and highly skilled Construction company specializing in civil & interior projects. Although the company is situated in Kottayam,Kerala we operate as far afield as Karnataka,Tamil Nadu & Middle East.. We are always available with the necessary skills, knowledge and equipment to meet any requirement in the field of general construction, civil construction, mechanical construction, electrical engineering and interior fitout works.
